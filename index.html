What I do

Three threads run through my work: learning algorithms grounded in physics, performance engineering on real hardware, and rigorous quantum theory with a strong publication record.

Machine Learning

ML & optimization for physical systems

Generative modeling with diffusion and energy-based models (Boltzmann machines), and Ising / combinatorial optimization. Currently building an amortized sampler for Boltzmann machines via correlated diffusion, realized on a continuous-variable optical Ising machine at Sandia National Laboratories.

GPU & HPC

CUDA performance engineering

Hand-optimized CUDA C++ kernels for scientific workloads. Built a low-power, field-deployable faint-object detection pipeline running in near real-time on quantum shot-noise-limited images, cutting runtime by ~108× on NVIDIA Blackwell.

Quantum

Quantum optics & photonics theory

Quantum field theory of structured light and first-principles light–matter interaction in crystals. 10 journal articles including a Phys. Rev. B Letter (Editor's Suggestion); co-author of the Purdue-PicoMax C++ electromagnetic simulation package.
